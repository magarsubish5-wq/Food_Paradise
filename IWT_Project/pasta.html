Creamy White Sauce Pasta

★★★★★ (350 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 25 Mins
Servings 4 People

"This is the richest, most flavorful white sauce pasta! The secret is infusing the milk with onion and cloves before cooking."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Penne Pasta
  • 1 cup Milk (Cold)
  • 2 tbsp Maida (All purpose flour)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Veggies: Corn, Capsicum, Carrot
  • Cheese Slices or Grated Cheese
  • Oregano & Chili Flakes
  • Black Pepper (Kali Mirch)

Instructions

Step 1

Boil water with salt and a drop of oil. Cook pasta until al dente (soft but firm). Strain and keep aside.

Step 2

In a pan, take 1 tsp butter. Sauté capsicum, corn, and carrots for 2 mins. Do not overcook; keep them crunchy. Remove from pan.

Step 3

In the same pan, melt 2 tbsp butter. Add 2 tbsp Maida. Roast it on low flame until it smells fragrant (don't burn it!). Slowly add cold milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps.

Step 4

Once the sauce thickens, add cheese, salt, pepper, oregano, and chili flakes. Add the sautéed veggies and boiled pasta. Mix well.

Step 5

Serve hot with Garlic Bread. Add extra cheese on top if you love it!
