Desi Kachumber Salad

★★★★☆ (223 reviews)
Prep Time 5 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 10 Mins
Servings 2 People

"The most refreshing side dish for spicy curries! The secret is soaking the chopped onions in ice water for 10 minutes to remove the harsh raw bite."

Ingredients

  • 1 Cucumber (Finely chopped)
  • 1 Onion (Finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (Finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (Optional)
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • Fresh Coriander leaves
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Chaat Masala (For the kick)

Instructions

Step 1

The secret to a good Kachumber is the size of the cuts. Chop the cucumber, onion, and tomatoes into very small, uniform cubes.

Step 2

In a large bowl, toss all the vegetables together. Add the chopped green chili if you like it spicy.

Step 3

Add salt, pepper, and Chaat Masala. Squeeze the lemon juice over it. Important: Do this right before serving, otherwise the onions will release water and the salad will get soggy.

Step 4

Throw in lots of fresh coriander leaves. Give it one final toss and serve immediately as a side dish with Dal Rice or Biryani.
