Traditional Sel Roti (Nepal Special)

★★★★★ (1250 reviews)
Prep Time Overnight
Cook Time ⏱️ 1 hours
Servings 4 People

"The taste of the Himalayas. Crispy sweet rice rings."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Raw Rice (Soaked overnight)
  • 1/2 cup Sugar (or to taste)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1/2 cup Water or Milk
  • 1 tsp Cardamom & Clove Powder
  • Oil or Ghee for deep frying
  • Optional: 1 Ripe Banana (for softness)

Instructions

Step 1

Wash the rice and soak it overnight (or at least 6-8 hours). Drain the water completely. Let the rice air dry for 15-20 minutes so excess moisture is gone.

Step 2

Grind the rice with sugar, ghee, spices, and the banana (if using) into a semi-fine paste. Do not make it too smooth; it should feel slightly grainy like semolina (sooji).

Step 3

Pour the batter into a large bowl. Add water/milk gradually to get a thick pouring consistency. Now, beat the batter with your hand for 10 minutes. This aerates it and makes the Sel Roti fluffy.

Step 4

Heat oil in a flat pan (not a deep kadhai). To pour the ring shape, you can use your hand (cup your palm) or use a funnel/plastic bottle with the bottom cut off. Pour a circle directly into the hot oil.

Step 5

Fry on medium heat. When one side is golden brown, flip it using a wooden stick or knitting needle (the traditional tool). Fry until crispy on both sides.
