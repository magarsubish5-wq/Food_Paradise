Crispy Eggless Waffles

★★★★★ (150 reviews)
Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time ⏱️20 min
Servings 4 People

"Golden, crunchy pockets ready for syrup or chocolate sauce."

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1/2 cup Corn Flour (Secret for crispiness!)
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1.5 cups Milk (Room temp)
  • 1/3 cup Melted Butter
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar or Lemon Juice (to make buttermilk)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence

Instructions

Step 1

Add vinegar/lemon juice to the milk and let it sit for 5 minutes until it curdles slightly. This replaces eggs and makes the waffles fluffy.

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together maida, corn flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Corn flour ensures the outer layer gets that satisfying crunch.

Step 3

Pour the buttermilk mixture, melted butter, and vanilla essence into the dry ingredients. Whisk gently until just combined. Small lumps are okay! Overmixing makes waffles chewy instead of crispy.

Step 4

Preheat your waffle iron. Brush it lightly with butter/oil. Pour a ladle of batter into the center (don't overfill). Close the lid.

Step 5

Cook for 4-5 minutes or until the steam stops coming out and the waffle is golden brown. Carefully remove it.
