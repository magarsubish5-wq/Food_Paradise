Punjabi Rajma Masala

★★★★★ (515 reviews)
Prep Time Overnight
Cook Time ⏱️45 min
Servings 4 People

"The King of Sunday lunches. Creamy kidney beans in thick gravy."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Red Kidney Beans (Rajma) - Soaked Overnight
  • 2 Onions (Finely chopped)
  • 3 Tomatoes (Pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • Spices: Turmeric, Red Chili, Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Butter/Ghee
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

Step 1

Soak Rajma for at least 8 hours. Pressure cook with salt and water for 5-6 whistles. The beans should be melt-in-mouth soft, not hard.

Step 2

Heat oil/ghee in a heavy pot. Add cumin seeds. Add onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 min.

Step 3

Add the fresh tomato puree. Add turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook this masala on medium heat until oil separates from the sides (approx 10-12 mins).

Step 4

Add the boiled Rajma along with its water to the masala. Pro Tip: Mash a few beans with the back of your ladle; this makes the gravy thick and creamy.

Step 5

Add Garam Masala and a dollop of butter. Simmer on low flame for 10 minutes to let flavors absorb. Garnish with coriander.

Step 6

Slit the Pav buns. Heat butter on the side of the pan, sprinkle some Pav Bhaji masala on the butter, and toast the Pav in it until warm and buttery.
