Classic Cheeseburger

★★★★★ (900 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 30 Mins
Servings 2 People

"No more soggy buns! The secret is toasting the cut sides of the bun with butter until they are golden brown and sealed."

Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Meat (Chicken/Lamb)
  • 1 Onion (Finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 Egg (for binding)
  • Breadcrumbs (optional)
  • 4 Burger Buns
  • 4 Cheese Slices (Cheddar)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato & Onion rings
  • Mayo, Ketchup & Mustard

Instructions

Step 1

In a large bowl, mix the minced meat, chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, salt, pepper, and the egg. Mix well with your hands. Shape them into round, flat patties (slightly larger than the bun as they shrink when cooking).

Step 2

Heat a little oil in a flat pan. Place the patties gently. Cook on medium heat for 4-5 minutes on each side until brown and cooked through.

Step 3

Just before taking the patty off the pan, place a cheese slice on top of each hot patty. Cover the pan with a lid for 1 minute to let the cheese melt perfectly.

Step 4

Slice the buns in half. Butter them lightly and toast them on the same pan until golden brown.

Step 5

Spread mayo and ketchup on the bottom bun. Place lettuce, then the cheesy patty, then tomato and onion rings. Cover with the top bun.
