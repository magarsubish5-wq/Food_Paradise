Eggless Chocolate Donuts

★★★★☆ (430 reviews)
Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time ⏱️2 Hrs (Proffing)
Servings 4 People

"Super soft fried dough dipped in rich dark chocolate."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1/2 cup Warm Milk
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Instant Yeast (or 1/2 cup Curd + 1 tsp Baking Soda)
  • 2 tbsp Melted Butter
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate (Chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Heavy Cream or Butter
  • Sprinkles (Optional)

Instructions

Step 1

Mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit for 10 minutes until it becomes frothy. (If using Curd/Soda method, skip this and mix curd directly with flour).

Step 2

In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and melted butter. Knead into a soft, slightly sticky dough for 10 minutes. Kneading is key for softness!

Step 3

Grease the bowl with oil. Place dough inside, cover with a damp cloth, and keep in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours. It should double in size.

Step 4

Punch down the dough to release air. Roll it out to 1/2 inch thickness. Cut out circles using a glass or cutter, and use a bottle cap to cut the small center hole.

Step 5

Let cut donuts rest for 20 mins. Heat oil on medium flame. Fry donuts until golden brown on both sides. They will puff up beautifully!
