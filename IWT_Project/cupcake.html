Eggless Vanilla Cupcakes

★★★★★ (350 reviews)
Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time ⏱️30 min
Servings 4 People

"Light, airy, and perfect for tea time or birthdays."

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1 cup Curd/Yogurt (Thick, room temp)
  • 3/4 cup Sugar (Powdered)
  • 1/2 cup Oil (Flavorless) or Melted Butter
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • Tutti Frutti or Choco Chips (Optional)
  • 100g Butter (Softened)
  • 2 cups Icing Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Milk
  • Vanilla Essence

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a muffin tray with paper cupcake liners. If you don't have liners, grease the molds with oil and dust with flour.

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk the Curd and Sugar until smooth and the sugar dissolves. Add Baking Soda and Baking Powder. Mix gently and set aside for 5 minutes. You will see bubbles appearing (this makes the cupcakes fluffy).

Step 3

Add the Oil and Vanilla Essence to the bubbly curd mixture. Mix well. Now, sieve the Maida directly into this wet mixture.

Step 4

Use a spatula to gently fold the flour into the wet mix. Do not overmix or the cupcakes will become tough. If the batter is too thick, add 1-2 tbsp of milk.

Step 5

Pour the batter into the liners (fill only 3/4th full). Bake for 20-25 minutes. A toothpick inserted in the center should come out clean.
