Gajar Ka Halwa

★★★★★ (420 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 40 Mins
Servings 4 People

"Dessert of the Month: Warm, rich, and loaded with nuts."

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Red Carrots (Grated)
  • 1 liter Full Cream Milk
  • 4 tbsp Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 cup Sugar (Adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp Cardamom Powder (Elaichi)
  • Chopped Nuts (Cashews, Almonds, Pistachios)
  • Khoya/Mawa (Optional, for richness)

Instructions

Step 1

Heat 2 tbsp Ghee in a heavy-bottomed Kadhai. Add the grated carrots and sauté for 5-8 minutes. This removes the raw smell and deepens the red color.

Step 2

Pour in the milk. Bring it to a boil, then lower the flame. Cook openly, stirring occasionally, until all the milk evaporates and is absorbed by the carrots. This takes patience (about 20-30 mins)!

Step 3

Once the mixture is thick, add sugar. The mixture will become liquidy again as the sugar melts. Continue cooking until it thickens again.

Step 4

Add the remaining ghee, cardamom powder, and Khoya (if using). Roast (bhunai) for another 5 minutes until the Halwa leaves the sides of the pan and looks glossy.

Step 5

Garnish with roasted nuts. Serve piping hot! It tastes even better the next day.
