Eggless Chocolate Cake

★★★★☆ (310 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 45 Mins
Servings 4 People

"Never bake a dry cake again! The secret is taking it out of the oven when a few moist crumbs still cling to the toothpick."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Curd/Dahi (Thick, not sour)
  • 1/2 cup Oil (Flavorless like Sunflower)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 1 cup Powdered Sugar
  • 1.5 cups Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder (Dark is best)
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 cup Milk (to adjust batter)

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a 6-inch round cake tin with oil and dust it with some Maida (or use butter paper).

Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk the Curd (Dahi) and Sugar until the sugar dissolves completely. Add the Oil and Vanilla Essence. Whisk well until it looks smooth and creamy.

Step 3

Place a sieve (chalni) over the bowl. Sieve the Maida, Cocoa Powder, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, and Salt directly into the wet mix. Sieving makes the cake airy.

Step 4

Gently mix the dry and wet ingredients using a spatula. Do not overmix or the cake will become hard. Add milk gradually if the batter is too thick. It should be ribbon consistency.

Step 5

Pour batter into the tin. Tap it twice on the counter to remove air bubbles. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Insert a toothpick in the center; if it comes out clean, it's done!
