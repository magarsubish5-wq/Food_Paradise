"Stop making sticky pasta! Learn the secret to the perfect Al Dente bite."

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Pot (The bigger, the better)
  • 200g Pasta (Penne, Fusilli, or Spaghetti)
  • 2 liters Water (Lots of water!)
  • 1 tbsp Salt (Generous amount)
  • A Timer
  • A Colander (Strainer)

Instructions

Step 1: The Rolling Boil

Fill a large pot with water. Place it on high heat. Wait until the water is boiling aggressively (big bubbles). [Image of boiling water pot]

Step 2: Salty Like the Sea

Once boiling, add the salt. The water should taste salty like sea water. This is the only chance to flavor the pasta itself.

Step 3: The Drop

Add the pasta. Stir immediately for 30 seconds so it doesn't stick to the bottom. Do not cover the pot.

Step 4: The 'Al Dente' Test

Check the package time (usually 8-10 mins). Start tasting 2 minutes before that time. Bite into a piece. It should be soft but have a tiny white firm center. This is 'Al Dente' (to the tooth).

Step 5: Magic Water

Crucial Step: Before draining, scoop out 1 cup of the starchy boiling water. Add this to your sauce later‚Äîit makes the sauce creamy and thick!

Step 6: Drain & Toss

Drain the pasta in a colander. Do not rinse with cold water (unless making salad) as it washes away starch. Immediately toss into your hot sauce.
