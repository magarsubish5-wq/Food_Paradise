Chatpata Jeera Aloo

★★★★☆ (56 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 10 Mins
Servings 2 People

"Get restaurant-style texture at home! The secret is shallow frying the boiled potatoes until golden brown before tossing them in the spices."

Ingredients

  • 4-5 Medium Potatoes (Boiled & Cubed)
  • 1 tbsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera) - The Hero!
  • 1 tsp Hing (Asafoetida)
  • 2 Green Chilies (Slit)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric & Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Amchur (Dry Mango Powder)
  • Fresh Coriander
  • Oil or Ghee

Instructions

Step 1

Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add cumin seeds (Jeera). Let them crackle and turn brown (don't burn!). Add Hing and green chilies.

Step 2

Keep flame low. Add turmeric and red chili powder into the hot oil. Stir quickly for 5 seconds (this releases color). Immediately add the potatoes.

Step 3

Toss the potatoes well so they are coated in the masala. Increase flame to high for 2 minutes to make the outer layer crispy.

Step 4

Sprinkle Amchur powder (or Lemon juice) and salt. Mix gently. Amchur gives that tangy "chatpata" taste.

Step 5

Turn off heat. Garnish with lots of chopped coriander. Serve with hot Phulka or Paratha.
