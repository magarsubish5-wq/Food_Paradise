Mo:mo with Red Chutney

★★★★★ (650 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time ⏱️ 45 Mins
Servings 2 People

"Thin skin, juicy filling, and fiery garlic chutney."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Water (for kneading)
  • 2 cups Cabbage (Finely grated)
  • 1 Carrot (Grated)
  • 1 Onion (Finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic (Minced)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • Black Pepper & Salt
  • 5-6 Dried Red Chilies (Soaked)
  • 2 Tomatoes (Blanched)
  • 6-7 Garlic Cloves
  • Vinegar & Soy Sauce

Instructions

Step 1

Boil tomatoes and dried red chilies for 5 minutes. Peel the tomatoes. Blend them with garlic, salt, a dash of vinegar, and soy sauce until smooth. This is your spicy dip!

Step 2

Mix maida, salt, and water to make a stiff dough (not soft like roti). Cover it with a damp cloth and rest for 20 mins. This makes rolling easier.

Step 3

Mix grated cabbage, carrot, onion, ginger-garlic, pepper, and soy sauce. Pro Tip: Add salt only right before filling, otherwise the veggies will release water.

Step 4

Roll the dough into small, very thin circles (edges should be thinner than the center). Put a spoonful of filling. Pinch and fold the edges to seal (pleat one side and stick to the other for a half-moon shape).

Step 5

Grease your steamer plate with oil. Arrange momos with gaps in between. Steam for 10-12 minutes. When they look shiny and translucent, they are done!
