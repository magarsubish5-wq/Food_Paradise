Classic Buttermilk Pancakes

★★★★★ (420 reviews)
Prep Time 10 Mins
Cook Time 15 Mins
Servings 4 People

These are the fluffiest pancakes you will ever make! The secret is letting the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Buttermilk
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Melted butter

Instructions

Step 1

In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk the buttermilk and eggs together. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Add the melted butter.

Step 3

Gently mix until just combined. It is okay if there are some lumps! Do not overmix or the pancakes will be rubbery.

Step 4

Heat a griddle or pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
