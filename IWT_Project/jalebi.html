Crispy Jalebi

★★★★☆ (900 reviews)
Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time ⏱️30 min
Servings 4 People

"Hot, crispy orange spirals soaked in saffron syrup."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  • 1 tbsp Corn Flour (for crunch)
  • 1/2 cup Curd (Sour is best)
  • A pinch of Baking Soda/Powder
  • Orange Food Color (or Turmeric)
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Saffron strands (Kesar)
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice (Prevents crystals)
  • Ghee/Oil for frying

Instructions

Step 1

Boil sugar and water. Add saffron and cardamom. Cook until it becomes slightly sticky (1-string consistency). Add lemon juice to stop it from solidifying. Keep it warm.

Step 2

In a bowl, mix maida, corn flour, and baking soda. Add curd and color. Mix well. Add water gradually to make a thick but flowy batter (ribbon consistency). Whisk it for 5 mins to aerate.

Step 3

Pour the batter into a ketchup squeeze bottle or a thick ziplock bag with a small hole cut at the corner. Cloth piping bags are traditional.

Step 4

Heat ghee/oil in a flat pan (Tawa). Keep flame medium. Squeeze the batter into the hot oil in concentric circles (spirals). Fry until crisp on both sides.

Step 5

Immediately dip the hot fried Jeri into the warm syrup. Let it soak for 30-40 seconds (flip once). Remove it before it gets soggy.
