Besan Laddu

★★★★★ (650 reviews)
Prep Time 10 min
Cook Time ⏱️30 min
Servings 6 People

"Melt-in-mouth balls of roasted gram flour and ghee."

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Besan (Gram Flour) - Coarse is better
  • 1/2 cup Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1 cup Powdered Sugar (Boora/Tagar)
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder (Elaichi)
  • Chopped Almonds & Pistachios
  • Raisins (Kishmish)

Instructions

Step 1

Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed Kadhai (wok) on low flame. Keep 1 tbsp ghee aside for later use.

Step 2

Add the Besan to the melted ghee. Roast it on low flame continuously. Stir constantly so it doesn't burn. It takes patience (about 20-25 mins).

Step 3

The mixture will loosen up and turn golden brown. A nutty aroma will fill your kitchen. If it looks too dry, add the reserved 1 tbsp ghee.

Step 4

Sprinkle a handful of water droplets over the roasted besan. It will bubble up. This creates a "danedar" (grainy) texture. Stir until water evaporates. Turn off the flame.

Step 5

Transfer the mixture to a plate. Let it cool until it is just warm to touch (not hot, or sugar will melt and become watery). Add sugar, cardamom, and nuts. Mix well with hands.

Step 6

Take a small portion and press tightly to form round balls. Garnish with a pistachio on top.
